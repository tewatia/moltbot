### [VISUAL: Show README hero image, then transition to architecture diagram]

## ‚ú® Key Features Overview

### [VISUAL: Feature highlights appearing one by one with icons]

**VOICEOVER:**

> "Let me give you a quick taste of what Clawdbot can do:
>
> **Multi-channel messaging** - Connect over 20 different messaging platforms. WhatsApp, Telegram, Slack, Discord, Signal, iMessage, Microsoft Teams, and more. One assistant, all your chats.
>
> **Voice interaction** - Talk to your assistant naturally with Voice Wake on Mac, iPhone, or Android. Say the wake word and start chatting.
>
> **Browser control** - Clawdbot can browse the web for you, take screenshots, fill forms, and automate tasks in a dedicated browser.
>
> **Live Canvas** - A visual workspace where your assistant can push content, show you information, and create interactive experiences.
>
> **Skills system** - Extend Clawdbot with over 50 built-in skills. Control your Philips Hue lights, play music on Spotify, manage your GitHub repos, take notes in Notion or Obsidian. The list goes on.
>
> **Multi-agent routing** - Run different AI agents for different purposes. A work agent, a personal agent, a family agent - each with their own context and capabilities.
>
> And all of this while your data stays on YOUR devices."

## üîí Privacy & Local-First Philosophy

### [VISUAL: Lock icon, "Your Device" graphic, contrast with cloud services]

**VOICEOVER:**

> "I want to emphasize something important here. Clawdbot is local-first. The Gateway - the brain of the system - runs on your machine. Your conversations, your files, your credentials - they stay with you.
>
> Now, of course, when you send a message to an AI like Claude or GPT, that message goes to their servers for processing. That's how AI works. But Clawdbot itself doesn't phone home. There's no Clawdbot Inc. collecting your data. It's open source, running on your hardware."
